Pat's salmon cookies offer the Pacific Northwest's (PNW) culture and cuisine all wrapped up in a cookie. What began as a personal holiday treat that Pat shared with his extended family throughout the world give them a simple taste of the PNW expanded to thousands of cookies sold each year at 5 stores, which includes 4 international locations. Pat's salmon cookies are locally and sustainably sourced.

We invite you to come enjoy the cookies that Food and Wine Magazine hailed as "the most unique cookies ever imagined to satisfy one's deepest morbid curiosity"
